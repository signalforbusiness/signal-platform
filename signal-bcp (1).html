Best Customer Profile

Signal correlates your customer intelligence with your account data to build a ranked spectrum of customer fit — from your highest-value relationships down to accounts worth monitoring. Each tier includes the specific profile, messaging that resonates, and the accounts that belong there.

What this module does and why it matters
Identifies who your best customers actually are — and builds a ranked spectrum you can use to target, qualify, and retain more of them.
  • → For Sales: Know exactly which prospects to prioritize and which to qualify out early — with a 100-point scorecard you can use in any first conversation
  • → For Marketing: Understand what messaging resonates with each customer tier and where to focus acquisition investment
  • → For Leadership: Make resourcing decisions based on which customers generate disproportionate long-term value — and which ones consume more than they're worth
  • → For the platform: BCP output feeds the ICP Stress-Tester (Module 03), the Churn Signal Detector (Module 05), and the Intelligence Brief (Module 06) — making every downstream module more precise
The instinct for who your best customers are usually lives in the head of one or two senior people. This module makes that judgment explicit, data-backed, and durable — so it belongs to the organization, not whoever happens to be holding it today.
