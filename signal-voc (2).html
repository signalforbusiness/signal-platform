What this module does
Turns raw customer research into ranked intelligence mapped to the Three Questions.
  • → Upload interviews, surveys, reviews, NPS comments, or support transcripts
  • → Signal extracts themes, scores them by frequency and intensity, and maps each to Q1, Q2, or Q3
  • → Every theme ends with a specific decision the business can act on — not just an observation
  • → Output feeds directly into the Best Customer Profile (Module 02) for deeper correlation
Use this module quarterly, or whenever you've gathered new customer research. The more sources you provide, the higher the confidence score.
