Why Signal

You already have the customer data.

Interviews, CRM notes, support conversations, NPS surveys, most growing companies have all of it already. What's missing is a system that turns it into a decision.

Who to target
Sales and marketing stop chasing accounts that just look promising and start going after the ones that actually expand and stick around. Signal shows which customer profiles drive real long term value, so pipeline and campaign spend follow evidence instead of gut feel.
Who to protect
Some accounts matter more than others, and most teams find out too late. Signal flags the relationships that would hurt most to lose, so retention effort goes where it actually matters.
Where to invest
Roadmaps often follow whatever request landed most recently, not what customers actually value. Signal shows which priorities really move retention and expansion, so investment goes where it counts.
What to change
Messaging often reflects internal assumptions more than customer reality. Signal surfaces the language customers use themselves, so positioning starts from evidence, not guesswork.
